About Me

I jump-started my love for design in high school when I realized I had a talent for computer arts. Throughout the years I created designs for my school district and after graduation went right into the Graphic Design and Web Development program at F.I.R.S.T. Institute. I learned a lot about the designing softwares and how to create many different works of art. On the sidelines, I was getting clients asking me to create their business logo's while I was working at an apparel printing shop. I can't wait to design more and see where my work takes me!
